üë§ About Me

Welcome to my homepage! I am a Research-Based MSc Computer Science graduate from the University of Calgary, where I conduct research in computational biology and machine learning. My work focuses on genomic data analysis and unsupervised learning methods, with particular interest in contrastive learning applications for genomic representation.

I am passionate about applying computational methods to solve biological problems and am always interested in collaborating with researchers working on related topics. Please feel free to reach out if you would like to discuss potential collaborations or have any questions about my work.
